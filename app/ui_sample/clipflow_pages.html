Features
Engineered for scale

The technical stack that powers the next generation of content production pipelines.

01
Decoupled Access
Editors upload to your secure firewall. You approve from yours. No more sharing YouTube passwords.
02
Zero Egress Fees
Built on Cloudflare R2. Move massive 4K files to YouTube server-to-server without paying a cent.
03
One-Click Publish
Review metadata, set visibility, and push to YouTube with a single tap. The file never touches your device.
04
Mobile Approval
Get notified when a video is ready. Preview and hit Publish from your phone, anywhere in the world.
05
Multi-Editor Teams
Invite unlimited editors with scoped upload-only access. Revoke in one click, no password resets required.
06
Full Audit Trail
Every upload, approval, and publish is timestamped and logged. Know exactly who did what and when.
Workflow
Three steps.
Zero compromise.
01
Editor uploads
Your editor gets a secure upload link. Drag-and-drop the finished video — no account, no credentials needed.
02
You review & approve
Get a notification. Open ClipFlow on any device, watch the preview, edit the metadata, then hit Approve.
03
We publish to YouTube
ClipFlow's servers push the file directly to your channel via YouTube API. Fast, secure, zero egress fees.
